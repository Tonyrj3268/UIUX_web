  • The interviewee exchanged currency at a physical bank and only exchanged US dollars.
  • The interviewee did not usually check the exchange rate but only started checking about two weeks ago and would not compare exchange rates at different banks.
  • The interviewee would not open a separate foreign currency account for exchange but still found it more convenient to communicate with people at a physical bank instead of online one.
  • The interviewee struggled to find information on how to open a foreign currency account on the bank's website and found the search function slow and confusing.
  • They preferred to open an account online if available.
  • The interviewee found the search function for finding physical bank locations convenient.
  • Overall, they gave the bank a score of 5 out of 10, citing difficulty finding the desired functions and the website's complexity. He would not recommend it to friends and relatives.
