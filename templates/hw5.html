作業五

撰寫 Personas 去分析我們的目標客戶並應用於設計當中

Here are the summary the feedback/comments/insights we received:

  1. The app panel design is easy to understand and supports other settings such as foreign exchange and languages. However, there are suggestions to revise the market plots and add a button to guide users from the exchange rate conversion to the actual exchange.
  2. The system has a cool feature of displaying a money flow table, but there are some areas for improvement such as adding prompts for personal information format, making the trade button more visible, and adding a button on the exchange page. Additionally, it would be helpful to provide notifications to users after completing a transaction.

Based on the feedback collected, here are some possible revisions to the Lo-Fi design we have came up with:

Main revisions:

  • Add a foreign exchange service to the app panel.
  • Support multiple languages in the app panel.
  • Revise the market plots on the index page.
  • Add a button to guide users from the exchange rate conversion to the actual exchange.

Additional revisions:

  • Improve the personal information format prompts.
  • Make the trade button more visible on the exchange page.
  • Add a notification feature for users after completing a transaction.
  • Further optimize the other functionalities of the app panel.

It's important to prioritize these revisions based on their impact and feasibility, and to test the revised Lo-Fi design with users to ensure that it meets their needs and expectations.
